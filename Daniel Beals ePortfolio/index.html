About me

Professional

Hello, I am a young Software Developer who is just finishing college at Indian Hills Community College. Motivation is always key when it comes to self improvement and desire to work. I feel motivation is one of my strongest strengths, and one of the more important skills I value above others. Some other skills I feel I have are leadership and a self starter. My leadership comes from experience from college with the club I was involved in for Computer Software Development. In this club I was voted among my peers to be president which I will explain below. A good work ethic is a skill I have always possessed, and constantly continue to improve on I have achieved this with good self-discipline and time management.

Being Helpful

Over my college years and at the end of high School, I found myself really enjoying helping people with issues around computers or anything techy. This got noticed in college and my instructor recommended me to tutor. I gladly took this offer and tutored during my college experience. To tutor and help people, you have to know your stuff. I feel this is by far the biggest reason I try to improve my skills. You can not help anyone if you, yourself, do not know the topic. If at any point I feel I can not help someone because of lack of knowledge, that is the worst feeling in the world and I try to change that by learning!

Personal

Music to set the mood

Hello, My name is Daniel Boyd Beals. Born and raised in Iowa, I am a farm boy, game loving guy who found himself in programming.I was born in Des Moines, and my family moved around the state frequently until we found ourselves homeless when I was in second grade. My grandparents took me in, and I moved to their farm while my mother worked on self-improvement. I mention this because this played a major part in influencing my life. This led me to having a very open mind throughout life.

Hobbies

So some of my hobbies include gaming and piano, along with some sports while I was in middle and high School. Some of these games Include Runescape and League of Legends. I feel that these games have had a large impact on my love for computers. I would always run into issues with my computer and I was the only one who could fix it. This lead to me having a really tech-savey nature and exploring a lot of things from hardware to software of computers.
